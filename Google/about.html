Who are we ?

Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. As a global technology company, we specialize in a wide range of internet-related products and services. From search to software to hardware, our goal is to build products that help people in their everyday lives.

The origin

Google began in 1998 as a search engine collaboration between two Stanford University Ph.D. students, Larry Page and Sergey Brin. They developed a new approach to online search, called BackRub, based on the principle that websites with more inbound links from other reputable sites should rank higher.

Growth and evolution

What began in a garage in Menlo Park, California, has grown into a global company with offices in more than 60 countries. Beyond search, we have expanded to develop and offer popular products like Gmail, Google Maps, the Android operating system, and the Chrome browser , read more about them here. This growth is fueled by our core belief that continuous innovation can make technology work better for everyone.

Our philosophy and values

Our founding philosophy is: "Focus on the user and all else will follow". This principle guides every product we build, ensuring that a seamless and helpful user experience is always the top priority.
